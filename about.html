Meet Cindy

✦ My Clay Journey ✦

I started working with polymer clay years ago, but eventually stepped away from it as life got busy. Last year, I found my way back to it and rediscovered how much I love creating again.

What started as a small creative hobby has grown into a passion for making handmade pieces inspired by cozy everyday things and nostalgic little details—from early Crumbl-inspired coasters to Lenox-style magnets, and now custom pins featuring flowers, playful characters, and everyday objects.
